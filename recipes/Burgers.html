Burgers

DESCRIPTION

A hamburger or simply burger is a food consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll

Ingredients

  • 70/30 Ground Beef
  • Brioche bun
  • Sliced cheese
  • Mayonaise

Instructions

  1. Roll ground beef into quarter pound balls
  2. Salt and pepper the balls of beef
  3. Sear and press the ground beef into a preheated pan
  4. place on bun with cheese and mayonaise
