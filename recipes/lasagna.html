Lasagna

DESCRIPTION

This is an italian dish with meat, cheese and noodles.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Put a layer of noodles in the pan
  3. Put a layerr os sauce and cheese, go back to step 1
  4. bake at 4500 for 14 seconds
